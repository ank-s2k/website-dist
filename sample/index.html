Engineering & Product

Collapsible Group Item #1

Product designer

Bangalore

Job brief

We are looking for a Product Designer to work on various products we develop for our customers.<br/>Product Designer responsibilities include defining product specifications, creating digital or print drawings and designing fully-functional products. To be successful in this role, you should have an eye for color and shape and be able to translate requirements into practical product features.<br/>Ultimately, you will help us build products that are easy to use and visually appealing to our potential customers.

responsibalities

Identify opportunities for new products<br/>Analyze how a new product ties in with market needs and consumer preferences<br/>Set design requirements based on briefs from internal teams and external partners<br/>Research materials and techniques.<br/>Sketch drafts on paper or digitally (for example, using CAD).<br/>Use 3D modeling software to design products and components.<br/>Produce prototypes and test functionality.<br/> Improve the design of existing products.<br/> Gather feedback from product users

requirements

Work experience as a Product Designer or similar role<br/>Experience in industrial design<br/>Creativity in mixing up colors, shapes and techniques<br/>Hands-on experience with computer-aided design (CAD) tools<br/>Good knowledge of 3D modeling software<br/>Experience with design programs (like Illustrator and Sketch)<br/>Time-management skills<br/>Adaptability<br/>BSc/MSc in Product Design, Manufacturing Design Engineering or relevant field
